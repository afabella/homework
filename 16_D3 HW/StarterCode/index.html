Correlations Discovered Between Age and Smoking

Findings reveal smoking is most frequent for ages 35- 41. The highest percentage of smoking being those who are in their later 30s. However, those in their early 30's showed they had a smaller percentage meaning they were less likely to smoke.

Several states had a higher percentage of smokers while a few had the least amount. States with higher percentage are West Virginia, Kentucky, and Arkansas. While the states with the least amount are Utah and California. Youngest smokers resided in Utah and oldest in Maine
